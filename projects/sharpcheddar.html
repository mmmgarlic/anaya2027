PROJECT BRIEF: Create a modular typeface & then create something with it!

PROJECT TITLE:

Sharp Cheddar (Say Cheese!)

TYPOGRAPHY TYPEFACE DESIGN ANIMATION & MOTION GRAPHICS

Sharp Cheddar is a playful, 70’s cartoon-inspired typeface that was initially designed in electric tape.

↑ Unmute me! ✷

I animated a title sequence as a type specimen for my typeface, to the song ‘Tank!’ composed by Yoko Kanno.

I was inspired by the song’s spy-fantasy feel, as well as by the works of Saul Bass for Hitchcock films like ‘Psycho’.
